Buttered Popcorn recipe:
1) Buy microwavable popcorn
2) Microwave the microwavable popcorn
3) Microwave butter
4) Make sure you have butter
5) Go back to the store to buy butter
6) Microwave the newly purchased butter
7) Dump liquid butter gold over now cold popcorn
8) Eat in grumpy silence.
9) Buy a new recipe book
